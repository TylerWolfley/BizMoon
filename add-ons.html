Add-Ons

Fixed-scope extras to keep building after your core launch.

Each add-on is a defined deliverable, not an open-ended service. Available after your BizMoon package is delivered. Ask about pricing and availability when you reach out.

How add-ons work

Add-ons are defined scope — not open-ended retainers.

Every add-on has a specific deliverable. Pricing and availability are confirmed at the time of request, not bundled into your package upfront.

Available add-ons

Four categories of fixed-scope extras.

Legal and setup

  • Expedited LLC filing support Research and step-by-step guidance for faster state filing — not legal advice.
  • Registered agent setup help Research and recommendations for your state. You select and pay the provider.
  • Insurance quote research A curated list of providers and coverage types relevant to your business.

Brand and design

  • Business card design Print-ready business card design consistent with your brand direction.
  • Flyer design One single-sided flyer design sized for print or digital distribution.
  • Branded email signature A clean, formatted email signature with your logo, contact details, and link.
  • Social media graphics pack Profile image, cover image, and 3 post templates sized for your chosen platforms.

Online presence

  • Google Business Profile optimization Review and update of posts, Q&A, photos, and profile completeness for an existing profile.
  • Extra website pages Additional pages added to an existing BizMoon site — copy, structure, and build included.
  • City pages for local expansion Up to 3 location-targeted service pages added to your site to support local search.

Content and operations

  • Estimate and invoice template pack A set of 3 formatted templates — estimate, invoice, and follow-up — ready to use.
  • Launch post captions 5 ready-to-post social captions written for your business launch announcement.
  • Monthly site update retainer One hour of text and content updates per month. Scope confirmed before each cycle.
