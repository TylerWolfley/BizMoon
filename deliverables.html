Core deliverables

Six things BizMoon builds — across packages.

Website

One-page landing site

A custom-built landing page with your business name, service description, service area, contact info, and a working lead capture form — live on your domain.

Forms

Branded contact or intake form

A configured contact form embedded in your landing page. Quote requests and inquiries arrive directly in your inbox from day one.

Email

Business email setup

A custom domain email address — yourname@yourbusiness.com — set up and ready to use. Looks more professional when you send your first estimate.

Graphics

Launch graphics

A profile image and cover image sized for Google Business Profile and Facebook. Ready to upload on launch day, consistent with your brand direction.

Google

Google Business Profile setup

Categories, service area, hours, description, and service list configured and submitted. Verification and rankings depend on Google.

Templates

Estimate and review templates

A formatted estimate template ready to send to your first quote request, plus review request messages ready to copy and text after completing a job.
