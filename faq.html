What happens first?

We start with intake and package fit. Turnaround targets begin after intake and materials are complete.

No. BizMoon is built for people who need help turning a rough service idea into something cleaner and customer-facing.

No. Where included, BizMoon provides setup and submission support, but verification depends on Google.

Are rankings guaranteed?

No. BizMoon does not guarantee ranking results. Google-related work is positioned as setup, submission support, and foundation work.

Can I add more later?

Yes. Add-ons are available if you want more launch help without jumping straight into a bigger package.

Do the packages include ongoing support?

Starter and Launch do not include ongoing support. Moonshot includes up to 14 days of post-launch support for minor edits.
