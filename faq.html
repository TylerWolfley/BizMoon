FAQ

Clear answers before you commit.

BizMoon keeps the process clean, the package boundaries clear, and the wording honest. These questions come up often — here are straight answers.

Trust note

Google Business Profile work is setup and submission support where included.

Verification and rankings are never guaranteed. This is stated clearly on every package page.

What happens first?

You reach out, BizMoon reviews fit, and I send a short intake form to confirm scope and package. Turnaround targets begin after intake and materials are complete — not from the day you inquire.

Do I need everything figured out before I start?

No. BizMoon is built for people who need help turning a rough service idea into something cleaner and customer-facing. If you have a name, a service, and a general area, that is enough to start.

Who actually does the work?

Tyler Wolfley does. There is no team, no account manager, and no subcontractor. When you reach out, you are communicating directly with the person who will build the landing page, configure your Google Business Profile, and deliver your files.

Is Google verification guaranteed?

No. Where included, BizMoon provides setup and submission support, but verification depends on Google. This is stated clearly on every package page and in every inquiry response.

What if I need changes after launch?

Moonshot includes up to 14 days of post-launch support for minor edits. Starter and Launch do not include ongoing support, but add-ons are available for continued help after delivery.

Are rankings guaranteed?

No. BizMoon does not guarantee ranking results. Google-related work is positioned as setup, submission support, and foundation work — not an SEO campaign.

How does payment work?

A deposit is required to begin work. The remaining balance is due before the finished deliverables are transferred. Payment details are confirmed during intake.

Can I add more later?

Yes. Add-ons are available if you want more launch help without jumping straight into a bigger package. Ask about what is available when you reach out.
