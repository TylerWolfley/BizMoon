FAQ

Straight answers before you commit.

Common questions about process, packages, scope, and what to expect. If something is not covered here, email hello@bizmoon.com.

Trust note

Google Business Profile work is setup and submission support where included.

Verification and rankings are never guaranteed. This is stated clearly on every package page.

What does the process look like?

You submit an inquiry, we reply within 1 business day. If it is a fit, a short intake form confirms the scope, deliverables, and timeline in writing. After intake and deposit, you provide materials and we build. Before launch, you review with up to 2 revision rounds. After approval, everything goes live and files are handed off. See the full process →

What do I need to provide?

After intake is confirmed, we send a simple asset checklist: business name, service list, service area, and any brand materials you already have (logo, colors, photos). If you have nothing yet, we work from your intake answers. You do not need everything figured out before you reach out.

Who actually does the work?

BizMoon handles every project directly. We keep the operation lean and communication direct — you always have a clear point of contact and know exactly what is being delivered and when.

Is Google verification guaranteed?

No. Where included, BizMoon provides setup and submission support, but verification depends on Google. This is stated clearly on every package page and in every inquiry response.

How do revisions work?

Before anything goes live, you receive a review link. You submit feedback and we make revisions. Each package includes up to 2 rounds of revisions on any deliverable. Revision scope follows the original intake — not open-ended changes.

What if I need changes after launch?

Moonshot includes up to 30 days of post-launch minor text edits. Starter and Launch do not include ongoing support, but add-ons are available for continued help after delivery.

How does payment work?

A 50% deposit is required to begin work. The remaining balance is due before the finished deliverables are transferred. Payment details are confirmed during intake.

Are rankings guaranteed?

No. BizMoon does not guarantee ranking results. Google-related work is positioned as setup, submission support, and a local optimization foundation — not an SEO campaign.
