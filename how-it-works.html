Process

Every BizMoon project follows the same seven-step workflow.

No surprises. No scope creep. No mystery about what happens next. Here is exactly how a BizMoon engagement runs from inquiry to handoff.

Timeline

Turnaround targets start after intake and materials are complete.

The clock begins once your intake form is signed and your deposit is received. Not from the day you inquire.

How it works

Seven steps, from inquiry to live.

  1. 1

    Submit your inquiry

    Use the contact form or email hello@bizmoon.com. Tell us what the business is, where you are in the process, and which package you are considering. No sales call required to get started.

  2. 2

    Fit review — response within 1 business day

    We review every inquiry and reply within 1 business day. If it is a fit, we confirm which package makes sense. If it is not the right time, we will tell you that too.

  3. 3

    Intake and scope confirmation

    If it is a fit, we send a short intake form that locks in the package, deliverables, and timeline in writing. A deposit is required to hold your spot. No work begins until intake is complete and signed.

  4. 4

    You provide your materials

    Once intake is confirmed, we send a simple asset checklist: business name, service list, location, and any brand materials you already have. If you have nothing yet, we work from your intake answers — you do not need to have everything figured out.

  5. 5

    Build

    The turnaround clock starts once materials are received. We handle the domain, copy, design, Google Business Profile setup, and all other package deliverables. You do not need to manage anything during the build.

  6. 6

    Review and revisions

    Before anything goes live, we share a review link. You look it over and submit feedback. Each package includes up to 2 rounds of revisions on any deliverable. Revision scope follows the original intake — not open-ended changes.

  7. 7

    Handoff and go-live

    After approval, the site goes live on your domain, the Google Business Profile is submitted where included, and all deliverable files are transferred to you. The business is online and ready for customers.

Revisions and scope

How revisions work — and what they cover.

  • Up to 2 revision rounds included

    Each package includes up to 2 rounds of revisions on any deliverable before launch. A revision round is your feedback plus the incorporated changes — back and forth, contained to the original scope.

    • Feedback submitted via review link
    • Changes applied within the stated turnaround window
    • Both revision rounds apply to any single deliverable

  • Revision scope follows intake — not open-ended changes

    Revisions are about refining the deliverables to match your intake. Adding new sections, changing the service offer, or requesting entirely different copy are out of scope.

    • Corrections and clarifications: in scope
    • New content, new sections: out of scope
    • Package scope changes require a new intake

  • What happens after launch

    Moonshot includes 14 days of post-launch minor text edits. Starter and Launch do not include ongoing support, but add-ons are available.

    • Moonshot: 14 days of minor text edits post-launch
    • Starter and Launch: no post-launch edits included
    • Add-ons available for continued help
