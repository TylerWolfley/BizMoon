What to expect

Submit an inquiry — it takes about 2 minutes.

Fill in the form with your business name, service type, and which package you are considering. That is enough to get started. We handle the rest.

1 — We review your inquiry

Every submission is reviewed promptly. We reply within 1 business day with either a fit confirmation or a direct answer about why the timing or scope is not right.

2 — Intake and scope lock

If it is a fit, we send a short intake form to confirm the package, deliverables, and timeline in writing. A 50% deposit secures your spot. Nothing starts before scope is agreed.

3 — You provide materials, we build

After intake, we send a simple asset checklist. You provide business details and any materials you have. We handle the domain, copy, design, and all package deliverables during the build phase.

Project inquiries only. BizMoon uses the details you share to review package fit, scope, and next steps.
