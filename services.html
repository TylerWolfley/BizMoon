Google Business Profile

Setup and submission support — included in Launch and Moonshot.

Google Business Profile is the most important free tool for local businesses. BizMoon configures your profile completely and guides you through the submission process.

  • What BizMoon sets up

    Category selection, service area, business description, hours, service list, and profile images — all configured and ready to submit.

    • Business category and sub-categories
    • Service area or address
    • Business description written for your business
    • Service list and pricing structure
    • Profile and cover images sized for Google

  • What BizMoon cannot guarantee

    Verification depends on Google's process and timeline. Rankings are not promised. BizMoon sets up the strongest possible foundation — Google decides the rest.

    • Verification timeline depends on Google
    • Rankings depend on many factors BizMoon does not control
    • Included in Launch and Moonshot only
