Website improvements

Updates, additions, new page sections, conversion improvements, and ongoing refinements to your existing site.

Branding and business assets

Quote templates, estimate documents, proposals, email signatures, branded presentation materials, and supporting visuals.

Visibility and SEO work

Content and messaging

Common questions

What to expect from BizMoon Orbit.

Is Orbit unlimited?

No. Orbit is a capacity-based system, not unlimited service. Each plan defines how much active work we can take on in a given month. Requests are handled in order of priority, and turnaround depends on task size and plan tier.

What kind of work fits Orbit?

Website improvements, page additions, on-page SEO work, brand and business assets, Google Business Profile content, document templates, messaging updates, and similar practical business-building tasks. See the full approved request list above for details.

Do I need a BizMoon launch package first?

Not necessarily. Orbit is designed primarily for businesses that have already launched, but we consider applicants on a case-by-case basis. If your business is already live and you want ongoing support, apply and we will review your setup.

Can I pause or cancel?

Orbit plans are month-to-month. You can cancel before the next billing cycle begins. Pausing may be available depending on your situation — ask when you apply.

How fast is turnaround?

Turnaround depends on task size and plan level. Orbit Lite has standard turnaround. Growth and Moon Mission have higher priority and faster cycle times. Larger tasks may be broken into stages across multiple cycles.

What if a task is too large for one cycle?

Larger builds are scoped and broken into stages. We handle what fits in a given cycle and continue in the next. Nothing gets dropped — it moves to the front of the next month's queue.
