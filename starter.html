BizMoon Starter

Go from idea to online — logo, website, email, and core setup done.

Starter is a focused, fixed-scope package with one goal: getting your business online fast and looking credible. We handle your starter logo, domain, a live multi-page website, branded email, basic SEO, and a branded document template — all organized and handed off in 5 business days.

Start with Starter Compare all plans

Turnaround

$999

Target: 5 business days after intake is complete.

Who it is for

A new business that needs to look credible and be reachable — without committing to a full launch build. Starter covers the essentials: a starter logo, domain, a multi-page website with a contact form, branded email, basic SEO setup, and a branded document template. If you want Google Business Profile setup and a more polished full-brand system, choose Launch.

What you walk away with

A starter logo or logo cleanup with favicon, domain connected to your site, up to 3-page website with contact form and mobile-optimized design, 1 branded business email setup, basic SEO metadata across your pages, 1 branded document template (estimate or invoice), and an organized delivery folder — all handed off in 5 business days.

  • Included

    What you get

    • One intake and business clarity session
    • Business name and offer positioning review
    • Starter logo or logo cleanup + favicon
    • Domain purchase included if available under $20
    • Domain connection and basic DNS setup
    • Up to 3-page website with contact form and clear CTA (pages defined at intake)
    • Mobile-optimized design
    • Basic SEO metadata across included pages (title, description, favicon)
    • 1 branded business email setup (where applicable)
    • 1 branded document template (estimate or invoice)
    • Organized delivery folder with login handoff doc
    • Up to 2 rounds of revisions on any deliverable

  • Not included

    What Starter does not cover

    • Full brand system or custom logo files
    • Pages beyond the 3-page limit confirmed at intake
    • Google Business Profile setup
    • Social profile setup (available as add-on)
    • Email signature (available as add-on)
    • Post-launch edits or support
    • Ongoing SEO, marketing, or ad management
