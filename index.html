How we operate

Defined scope. Fixed pricing. Direct communication.

Every project has a written scope, a clear timeline, and one person accountable for the build. No retainers, no ambiguity, no handoffs to a team.

Scope confirmed in writing

Before work begins, intake documents the exact deliverables, package, and timeline. What is included — and what is not — is stated clearly before you pay.

Fixed turnaround targets

Each package has a stated turnaround target. The clock starts after intake is complete and your deposit is received — not from the day you inquire.

How it works

Inquiry to handoff — a clear seven-step workflow.

Every BizMoon project follows the same process, from your first message to the day your business goes live.

  1. 1

    Submit your inquiry

    Use the contact form or email hello@bizmoon.com. No sales call required to get started.

  2. 2

    Fit review and intake

    Tyler responds within 1 business day. If it is a fit, a short intake form locks in the scope, deliverables, and timeline in writing.

  3. 3

    Build, review, and go live

    Tyler builds everything. You review with up to 2 revision rounds. After approval, the site goes live and all files are handed off.
