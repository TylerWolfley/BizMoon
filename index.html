Website

Built around your services, your service area, and one primary call to action. Hosted and live at handoff, not delivered as a file to configure later.

Brand assets

How every build is delivered

A clear process, not a black box.

Every BizMoon project runs through the same three steps, in the same order, so nothing falls through between intake and handoff.

  1. 1

    Intake and brief

    You fill out a short intake form with your business name, services, and key details. We review it, clarify anything we need, and confirm scope before starting.

  2. 2

    Build and review

    We build your site, set up your accounts, and handle the technical connections. You get a review link before anything goes live.

  3. 3

    Handoff and done

    Once approved, everything goes live and you receive a handoff folder with your logins, files, and a documented record of what was set up.
