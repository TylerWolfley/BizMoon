How it works

Every BizMoon project follows the same seven-step workflow.

  1. 1

    Submit your inquiry

    Use the contact form or email hello@bizmoon.com. Tell me what the business is, where you are in the process, and which package you are considering. No sales call required to get started.

  2. 2

    Fit review — response within 1 business day

    I review every inquiry personally and reply within 1 business day. If it is a fit, I confirm which package makes sense. If it is not the right time, I will tell you that too.

  3. 3

    Intake and scope confirmation

    If we move forward, I send a short intake form that locks in the package, deliverables, and timeline in writing. A deposit is required to hold your spot. No work begins until intake is complete and signed.

  4. 4

    You provide your materials

    Once intake is confirmed, I send a simple asset checklist: business name, service list, location, and any brand materials you already have. If you have nothing yet, I work from your intake answers — you do not need to have everything figured out.

  5. 5

    Build

    The turnaround clock starts once materials are received. I handle the domain, copy, design, Google Business Profile setup, and all other package deliverables. You do not need to manage anything during the build.

  6. 6

    Review and revisions

    Before anything goes live, I share a review link. You look it over and submit feedback. Each package includes up to 2 rounds of revisions on any deliverable. Revision scope follows the original intake — not open-ended changes.

  7. 7

    Handoff and go-live

    After approval, the site goes live on your domain, the Google Business Profile is submitted where included, and all deliverable files are transferred to you. The business is online and ready for customers.
