About BizMoon

We exist to close the gap between having a business idea and being live online.

Most new businesses spend weeks stuck on setup — domains, copy, design, Google profiles — instead of finding their first customers. BizMoon fixes that.

How we operate

Defined scope. Fixed pricing. Direct communication.

Scope is confirmed in writing before work begins. Communication is clear, and the deliverables are stated before you pay.

The mission

The cleaner, faster path more business owners deserve from day one.

BizMoon was built for entrepreneurs and new business owners who have a real offer to sell, but no time to figure out the technical side of getting online. The problem is consistent across business types: setup takes longer than it should, and that delay costs momentum.

Every package has a defined deliverable set, a clear timeline, and a fixed price. No retainers, no surprises, no open-ended billing. The goal is to get your business online — credible, professional, and ready for customers.
