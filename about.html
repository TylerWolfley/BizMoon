The story

BizMoon is the cleaner, faster path I wish more business owners had found sooner.

I work directly on every BizMoon project. There is no team, no account manager, and no subcontractor doing the build while I field emails. When you reach out, you are talking to the person who will set up your domain, write your landing page copy, and configure your Google Business Profile.

I built BizMoon for new business owners who have a real skill or offer to sell, but no time to figure out the technical side of getting online. The problem is the same across most early-stage businesses: setup takes longer than it should, and that delay costs momentum. Cleaners, contractors, landscapers, and handymen are a strong fit — and so are most entrepreneurs starting from scratch.

BizMoon is intentionally scoped. Every package has a defined deliverable set, a clear timeline, and a fixed price. No retainers, no surprises, no open-ended billing. The goal is to get your business online — credible, professional, and ready for customers — without the setup delays that hold most new owners back.

How I operate

Defined scope. Fixed pricing. Direct communication.

Scope confirmed in writing

Before work begins, intake documents the exact deliverables, package, and timeline. What is included — and what is not — is stated clearly before you pay.

Fixed turnaround targets

Each package has a stated turnaround target. The clock starts after intake is complete and your deposit is received — not from the day you inquire.

Honest Google language

Google Business Profile work is setup and submission support. Verification depends on Google. Rankings are not promised or implied on any package page.

One point of contact

Tyler handles the work and the emails. There is no account manager between you and the build. Questions go directly to the person doing the work.
