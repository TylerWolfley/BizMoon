Common questions

What to expect from a Launch Snapshot.

What is a Launch Snapshot?

It is a short custom review of your current business setup. We look at what you have, identify the gaps that matter most, and tell you what BizMoon would recommend doing first. It is a written review, not a free build or design session.

Is this free?

Yes. The Launch Snapshot is completely free. There is no obligation, no invoice, and no sales call required to receive it. You submit a form, we review it, and we send back our assessment.

Does this include build work?

No. The Launch Snapshot is a review only. It does not include any design, development, copywriting, or setup work. If a BizMoon package is a fit after the review, that is a separate engagement.

How long does it take to hear back?

We review submissions as they come in. Response time depends on current volume, but we aim to send snapshots in the order they arrive. You will hear from us — typically within a few business days.
